Was Ringo Starr the original Beatles drummer?
No, Pete Best was the original drummer.
When did Ringo join them?
Ringo joined the band in August 1962 after Best was fired.
What songs did Ringo sing on?
He sang lead vocals on 11 of them including Yellow Submarine, Octopus Garden and With a Little Help from My Friends.
Did Ringo write any of the Beatles songs?
He was the write or co writer on 10 songs including Maggie May, What Goes On and Flying.
Was Ringo Starr his real name?
No, Ringo was born Richard Starkey on July 7, 1940.
