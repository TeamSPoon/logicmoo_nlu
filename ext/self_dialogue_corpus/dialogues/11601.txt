Do you ever listen to The Beatles' music?
All the time, they were one of my favorite bands.
Which one of the members do you think was the most talented?
They were all great, just in different ways.  Paul McCartney and John Lennon were both great singers, and songwriters.
Which one of The Beatles do you think was the best on his instrument?
Definitely George Harrison, he was a great guitar player.
Do you have a favorite Beatles song written by George Harrison?
While My Guitar Gently Weeps, one of my favorite songs by any band.
Did you know that Eric Clapton played on While My Guitar Gently Weeps?
I did know that. Apparently George Harrison and Eric Clapton were lifelong friends, despite having been married to the same woman.
