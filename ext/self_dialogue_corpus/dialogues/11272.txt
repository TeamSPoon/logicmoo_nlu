Can you tell me anything about the Beatles Hamburg,Germany experience?
Sure,they played there from 1960 to 62.
Why was this a crucial period for them?
They played there for hours which honed their skills to get to be the best.
Didn't a member die there?
Yes, sadly Stu Sutcliffe died of a brain hemorage there at the young age of 21.
What about Pete Best?
He was the drummer who played in Hamburg for the Beatles. Ringo came along and replaced him a little later.
Anything about The Top Ten andThe Star-Club?
They were the main clubs the Beatles performed in during the Hamburg period.  Historically, I would love to go back in a time machine to see the Beatles play those clubs.
