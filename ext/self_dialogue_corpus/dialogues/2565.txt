I just saw highlights from Superbowl XXVIII.
Who played in that one?
Dallas versus Buffalo.
Who were the quarterbacks?
Troy Aikman for Dallas and Jim Kelly for Buffalo.
Where was it played?
The Georgia Dome in Atlanta.
Who were the broadcasters?
Dick Enberg and Bob Trumpy.
Who sang the National Anthem?
Natalie Cole.
What was the halftime score?
13-6, Buffalo, actually.
Who played at halftime?
A lot of country singers - Clint Black, Tanya Tucker, Wynonna and Naomi Judd.
How did the third quarter go?
Dallas scored 14 points, unanswered.
And the fourth?
Dallas scored ten unanswered points.
Buffalo lost the Superbowl four times!
