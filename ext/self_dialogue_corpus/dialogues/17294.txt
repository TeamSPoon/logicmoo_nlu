Are you a fan of the Grateful Dead?
Oh yeah, They were a great band!
Have you ever been to any of their concerts?
Yes. I have been to at least 10 of them.
My mom and dad were dead heads and used to follow them around. That's how I got into them.
What are some of your favorite songs from them?
Sugar Magnolia is my favorite but I also like Ripple, Friend of the Devil and Hell in a Bucket.
Those are awesome songs! My personal favorite is Uncle Johns Band.
How many albums have they made?
They made more than 140 albums and most of them were live recordings. They only had 13 studio albums.
What was their first and last albums?
Their first was recorded in 1967 and was titled The Grateful Dead. Their last was recorded in 1989 and was titled Built to Last.
How many concerts did they perform?
During their long strange strip, they did 2,317 live concerts. They preferred live concerts over recording in a studio.
Did they ever have a Top 10 song?
No they never did. They did however have a one and only Top 40 hit which was Touch of Grey. It hit number 9 on the Billboard Hot 100.
Did they ever win any awards?
In 2007 they won the Grammy Lifetime Achievement Award, but never an actual Grammy. In 1994 they won the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame  Performer for Spring 1990 (The Other One).
What was their best selling album?
Skeletons from the Closet: The Best of Grateful Dead was their best selling album. It was certified triple platinum.
