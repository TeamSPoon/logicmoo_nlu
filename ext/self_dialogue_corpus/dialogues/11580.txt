How many albums did The Beatles record?
They recorded 54 compilation albums, 28 studio albums, 11 video albums and 6 live albums.
Did The Beatles have any Top 10 albums?
They has quite a few including Abbey Road, Sgt. Peppers Lonely Hearts Club Band and Rubber Soul.
How many Beatles songs include the name of a woman?
17 songs have the name of a woman in them including Eleanor Rigby, Lucy in the Sky with Diamonds and Lovely Rita.
What Beatles song spent the longest time on the charts?
Hey Jude holds the record for the longest song on the charts. It stayed there for 19 weeks.
Did The Beatles ever win a Grammy Award?
Yes they won 9 of them. Among them were for Michelle, Let It Be and Free as a Bird.
