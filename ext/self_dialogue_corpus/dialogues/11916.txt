Can you tell me about when the Beatles played in Nippon Budokan Hall in Japan?
Sure can. The Beatles played 5 dates there between June 30th and July, 2 1966.
How many songs were in the sets?
Each set had 11 songs clocking in at about a half hour.
Which song did they end the show with?
I`m Down which drove the crowds nuts.
There was a rumor that some Japanese didn't want the Beatles there?
True,some elders were upset the Beatles set a bad example for Japanese youth by leading them away from the traditional Japanese values.
Was there any film of this so I can watch it?
Yes,luckly The shows were filmed by Nippon Television, and broadcast as The Beatles Recital From Nippon. You can find it on Amazon or You-Tube.
